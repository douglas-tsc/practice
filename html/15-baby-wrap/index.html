The World's Most Comfortable Baby Wrap

Product Description

The cool, lightweight KangaWrap baby carrier holds up to 15kgs and is the perfect introduction to the fast growing world of baby carrying.

The KangaWrap baby sling is designed to hold your baby close straight from birth, KangaWrap meets Kangaroo Care criteria. The KangaWrap was developed especially for kangaroo care & skin-to-skin contact using award-winning research carried out by midwives alongside Trade4Life.
