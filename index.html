Introducing the Digipak System

The machine is capable of handling almost any tray products, including Digipak®, DVD, Pozzoli, Surepak, Paperfoam, Repack and Viva. Using the RP Serier placer, the system can achieve speeds of 150 products per minute and with the brushless servo technology means registration of ± 0.5mm.
