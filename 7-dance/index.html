Tradicion y Cultura, Inc.


Academia de Baile is a non-profit organization located in Tucson, AZ. Students have the opportunity to learn traditional Hispanic Dances, as well as classical and contemporary styles of dance. Classes offered include: Folklorico, Flamenco, Ballet, Tap, Jazz, and Hip Hop.

Students come to class once a week for forty-five minutes, from September through June. All students participate in an end of the year Dance Recital where they showcase what they’ve learned. Each group learns two dances: the first is a traditional Folklorico dance from Mexico, or a Flamenco dance from Spain. The second dance can be ballet, tap, jazz, or hip hop, depending on the themes for the Recital. Groups are determined by age and dance experience. Classes begin at age four, and we tailor to children of all ages.
